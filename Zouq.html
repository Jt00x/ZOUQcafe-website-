Our Story

At ZOUQ Café, we’re more than just a coffee spot — we’re a place where culture, flavour, and community meet. Inspired by the Arabic word “zouq” meaning taste and style, our café brings a unique twist to Bray’s dining scene

Since opening in March 2025, ZOUQ has become a local favourite, known for our specialty coffees, innovative matcha creations, and freshly prepared dishes made with love.

Every dish tells a story, every cup is crafted with care, and every visit feels like coming home.
